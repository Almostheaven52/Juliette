Original Samsung Timer

The image above is a screenshot of the timer that comes pre installed on samsung phones. It's servicable but it could be better. Its designed like a scroll wheel where you scroll to pick a value between 0 and 60.The main issue is that it isnt very precise. It is very easy to accidentally scroll to the wrong number or change a value by accident. It's also just kind of annoying to have to scroll through all those numbers.
